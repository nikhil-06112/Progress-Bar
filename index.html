Amer Fort , Jaipur

Amer Fort, located in Jaipur, India, is a majestic 16th-century fortress renowned for its artistic Hindu architecture. Overlooking Maota Lake, it boasts intricately carved marble and red sandstone, ornate halls, and stunning frescoes. Highlights include the Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace) and the Diwan-i-Khas. A UNESCO World Heritage site, it reflects Rajasthan's royal heritage.
